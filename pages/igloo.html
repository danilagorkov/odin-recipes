Igloo Meat Loaf

I don't know who came up with this but that person was crazy

Ingredients for Loaf

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons instant minced onion
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1-1/3 cups evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 6 tablespoons instant minced onion
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Ingredients for Potato Frosting

  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 2/3 cups evaporated milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 envelope (3-1/2 oz.) instant mashed potatoes

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a bowl lightly mix ground beef, crumbs, onion, salt, pepper and evaporated milk just until blended.
    Pack firmly into a 1-1/2-quart bowl; turn out of bowl onto a shallow baking pan.
  2. Bake in a preheated 325 degree oven 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 30 minutes.
  3. To prepare Potato Frosting: In a 1 -1/2-quart saucepan combine water, evaporated milk, salt and butter; bring just to boiling.
  4. Remove from heat; with whip or fork stir in potatoes; whip briskly. Frost loaf with potato mixture.
  5. Bake an additional 15 minutes. Place Cheddar cheese slices on top of Igloo; return to oven just until cheese starts to melt.
